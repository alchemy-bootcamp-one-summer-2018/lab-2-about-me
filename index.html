A Brief Biography

I grew up in a very rural area of upstate New York, on a dirt road 8 miles outside of the nearest tiny town. I lived about a mile up a hill from the this covered bridge.

I studied Physics and Astronomy for a couple of years at SUNY StonyBrook.

Then I studied creative writing at SUNY Oswego...

...and Eastern Washington University.

Fun Fact

Recluse? Woman of Mystery?

It's very difficult to find a picture of me by searching my name on the internet, not because they aren't out there, but because my name is so common. You need more specific knowledge about me to find anything. For example, if you search "Sarah Flynn NRAO" you will find pictures of dubious quality from a research assitantship I did at the National Radio Astronomy Observatory 16 years ago.

In many of them my eyes are closed.

Some are a bit askew.

In some I'm wearing a hard hat.

Or some combination of the above...
